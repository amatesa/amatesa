# 00 — Alejandro DNA

## Purpose

This document is the single source of truth describing who Alejandro is as a professional.

It is **not** intended to be published.

It exists so that every future document, portfolio page, LinkedIn profile, résumé, project description, presentation or AI assistant starts from the same understanding.

This document should remain more stable than any individual project or job experience.

---

# Core Identity

Alejandro is a problem solver before he is a specialist.

He is not defined by a programming language, a framework, a methodology or an industry.

He is defined by the way he approaches unfamiliar situations.

His natural instinct is to understand a system before attempting to improve it.

---

# Fundamental Motivation

Alejandro is primarily motivated by progress.

He enjoys understanding how things work and making them work better.

Learning is an essential part of that process because every new challenge expands the range of problems he can solve.

At the same time, he values recognition earned through meaningful work.

He does not seek visibility for its own sake, but he finds genuine satisfaction in knowing that his work made a difference and that others recognize its quality.

Improvement is not only something he pursues for himself.

He wants the results of that improvement to be useful, noticeable, and remembered.

---

# Relationship with Technology

Technology is a tool.

Never the objective.

Alejandro enjoys learning new technologies because they allow him to solve new kinds of problems.

If tomorrow those technologies disappeared, he would continue solving problems with whatever tools remained available.

His value is independent from the tools he uses.

---

# Relationship with Learning

Learning is not a separate activity.

Learning is part of working.

Every unfamiliar project represents an opportunity to expand his understanding.

He does not wait until he knows everything before starting.

He starts, learns, adapts and improves continuously.

---

# Problem Solving Process

His natural process is remarkably consistent across different industries.

Observe.

Read.

Understand.

Question assumptions.

Break the problem into smaller parts.

Identify the real cause instead of the visible symptom.

Build a practical solution.

Evaluate the result.

Improve it.

Repeat.

This process appears in healthcare, fintech, web development, data analysis and game development alike.

---

# Decision Making

Alejandro rarely jumps directly to conclusions.

He prefers gathering context first.

Documentation.

Processes.

People.

Existing constraints.

Only after understanding the environment does he propose changes.

---

# Curiosity

His curiosity is systematic rather than impulsive.

He asks:

Why does this work?

Why is it done this way?

Can it be simplified?

What problem is this actually solving?

His curiosity usually produces documentation, experiments or process improvements.

---

# Relationship with Complexity

Complexity does not intimidate him.

His first instinct is to decompose it.

He naturally transforms large problems into smaller and more manageable pieces.

Once those pieces become understandable, he starts rebuilding the complete picture.

---

# Strengths

Rapid learning.

Structured thinking.

Systems thinking.

Adaptability.

Documentation.

Pattern recognition.

Process improvement.

Cross-disciplinary understanding.

Persistence.

Analytical reasoning.

---

# Natural Behaviors

When joining a new environment, he first observes.

When facing a problem, he questions the apparent cause.

When learning a tool, he wants to understand the underlying concepts.

When finishing a project, he immediately sees opportunities for improvement.

When repeating the same task for too long, his motivation decreases.

---

# Relationship with Perfection

Alejandro is highly self-critical.

He often underestimates his own work because he compares it with specialists.

This tendency sometimes prevents him from recognizing the value of combining multiple disciplines.

His objective evaluation should always prioritize evidence over self-perception.

---

# Relationship with AI

Artificial Intelligence is an amplifier.

Not a substitute.

Alejandro uses AI to validate assumptions, accelerate learning and explore alternatives.

He remains responsible for understanding the final solution.

AI increases his range.

It does not replace his judgment.

---

# Professional Positioning

Alejandro should never be presented as:

"The expert who knows everything."

Instead he should be presented as:

"The professional who understands unfamiliar problems quickly and builds thoughtful solutions."

---

# Communication Style

Clear.

Honest.

Structured.

Reflective.

Evidence-based.

Never exaggerated.

Never artificially confident.

Never apologetic.

---

# Values

Understand before acting.

Improve before replacing.

Document before forgetting.

Learn before assuming.

Build before claiming.

Share before keeping.

---

# Evidence

His career consistently demonstrates the same behaviors despite changing industries.

Healthcare.

Operations.

Fintech.

Data Analysis.

Web Development.

Artificial Intelligence.

Game Development.

The environments changed.

His way of working did not.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Alejandro wants to build digital products that improve the way people work, learn or interact.

He is interested in creating systems that remain useful over time rather than chasing temporary trends.

His long-term goal is not to master a single discipline.

It is to become someone capable of connecting disciplines to solve increasingly complex problems.

---

# Legacy

If someone describes Alejandro after working with him, the desired conclusion is not:

"He knew many technologies."

The desired conclusion is:

"He understood the problem, improved the system and left it better than he found it."

---

# Non-Negotiable Principles

Never describe Alejandro through a list of tools.

Never exaggerate experience.

Never fabricate expertise.

Never use generic motivational language.

Never prioritize appearance over substance.

Never optimize for trends at the expense of authenticity.

Always communicate curiosity through evidence.

Always communicate adaptability through examples.

Always communicate learning through action.

---

# Guiding Sentence

Everything created from this document should reinforce one simple idea:

Alejandro is the kind of professional who understands complex problems, learns what is necessary, and leaves every system better than he found it.
