# 19 — User Journey

## Purpose

This document describes the complete journey visitors experience while exploring the portfolio.

It defines progression.

Not pages.

Not components.

The portfolio should feel like a guided discovery rather than a sequence of independent screens.

---

# Overall Journey

The visitor never jumps between unrelated sections.

Every chapter answers one question while naturally creating the next.

The experience is continuous.

The visitor should always know where they came from.

Where they are.

And why they want to continue.

---

# Chapter 01

HOME

Question

"Who is this?"

Answer

Someone who thinks differently.

Creates curiosity.

Nothing more.

The visitor should leave wanting context.

---

# Chapter 02

THINK

Question

"How does this person approach problems?"

The interface demonstrates observation.

Relationships appear.

Systems organize themselves.

The visitor experiences understanding before reading about it.

At the end of this chapter, one conclusion should naturally emerge:

"This is how he thinks."

Which leads to the next question.

"What has he built using that way of thinking?"

---

# Chapter 03

BUILD

Question

"What happens when this mindset becomes work?"

Projects appear as evidence.

Not trophies.

Each project answers:

What was the challenge?

What changed?

What was learned?

Technology remains secondary.

Every project reinforces the same narrative established earlier.

Understanding.

Structure.

Improvement.

The visitor now asks:

"Where did these skills come from?"

---

# Chapter 04

LEARN

Question

"How was this way of thinking developed?"

Learning is represented as a network.

Not a timeline.

Connections become visible.

Healthcare.

Operations.

Data.

Automation.

Artificial Intelligence.

Game Development.

Each discipline strengthens another.

The visitor realizes there is no career change.

Only continuous evolution.

The next question becomes:

"What else is he exploring?"

---

# Chapter 05

EXPLORE

Question

"What is he curious about now?"

This chapter feels like opening drawers in a workshop.

Small experiments.

Concepts.

Ideas.

Academic projects.

Future directions.

Nothing feels finished.

Everything feels alive.

Curiosity becomes contagious.

The visitor naturally wonders:

"What would it be like working with him?"

---

# Chapter 06

CONNECT

Question

"How do I continue this conversation?"

This chapter feels quiet.

No selling.

No pressure.

No forms asking for unnecessary information.

Simply clear ways to continue the relationship.

LinkedIn.

GitHub.

Email.

Resume.

The experience ends calmly.

Confidence replaces persuasion.

# Information Density

The portfolio gradually increases complexity.

The first screen is simple.

Each chapter introduces one additional layer.

Visitors never receive everything at once.

Understanding grows naturally.

---

# Narrative Rules

Every chapter answers exactly one major question.

Every answer creates the next question.

Nothing interrupts this flow.

Nothing exists outside this narrative.

---

# Visitor Outcome

When leaving the portfolio, visitors should remember:

Not the animations.

Not the technologies.

Not the color palette.

Instead, they should remember one idea:

"This is someone who understands systems, improves them, and enjoys building better ways of working."

If that idea remains after closing the browser, the experience has succeeded.

---

# Final Principle

The visitor should never feel like they finished reading a portfolio.

They should feel like they finished understanding a person.
